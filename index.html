Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" is a personal finance and self-help book written by Robert T. Kiyosaki. The book contrasts the financial philosophies and approaches of two father figures in Kiyosaki's life: his biological father (referred to as the "Poor Dad") and the father of his childhood best friend (referred to as the "Rich Dad").
